# Cloud Pricing

Simple, transparent pricing for the VisionPipe Cloud API.

## How It Works

VisionPipe uses a **credit-based** system:

- **1 credit = 1 API call**
- Credits never expire
- No monthly fees or subscriptions
- Pay only for what you use

## Billing FAQ

### How do I buy credits?

1. Go to [Billing](/c/cloud/billing)
2. Select a credit package
3. Complete checkout with Stripe
4. Credits are added instantly

### Do credits expire?

No, credits never expire. Use them whenever you need.

### Can I get a refund?

Unused credits can be refunded within 30 days of purchase. Contact support@visionpipe.com.

### How do I check my balance?

View your credit balance at [Billing](/c/cloud/billing) or [Usage](/c/cloud/usage).

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- And more

### Is there an invoice?

Yes, invoices are automatically generated and available in your Stripe customer portal.
